Promotion Details

Nokia’s promotional strategies before the advent of the internet focused on innovation and reliability, highlighting their role in pioneering mobile technology.They emphasized their contributions to telecommunications, such as the development of the GSM standard and their mobile phones which were known for their durability and long battery life. Nokia’s marketing campaigns often showcased the functionality and design of their products, appealing to a broad audience by promoting a lifestyle of connectivity and ease of use. As they expanded globally, Nokia’s brand became synonymous with trustworthy and cutting-edge technology

About us

Nokia was known for a variety of products. One of the most notable was its line of mobile telephones, particularly the Nokia 1011. Introduced in 1994, the Nokia 1011 was among the first mass-produced digital phones. Nokia set a sales target of 400,000 units for its 2100 series but ended up selling 20 million handsets worldwide1. This success helped establish Nokia as a household name in mobile technology.
